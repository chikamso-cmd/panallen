JOIN OUR TEAM

Welcome to the Careers page of our cluster, a dynamic and innovative leader at the intersection of data and energy. We are driven by a passion for harnessing the power of data to create sustainable energy solutions that shape the future. If you're looking for a career where you can make a real impact, collaborate with top talents in the industry, and be part of cutting-edge projects, you've come to the right place.

Why join us?

Purpose-Driven Work: Our work goes beyond just business; it's about addressing global challenges and contributing to a cleaner, smarter, more+ sustainable world and profitable businesses. With us, every project you work on will have a meaningful impact on the data and energy landscape.

Innovation and Technology: We thrive on innovation. As part of our team, you'll be at the forefront of ground-breaking technologies, using data-driven insights to drive transformation and reshape businesses and individual skills and knowledge.

Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration. You'll be working alongside diverse experts from various disciplines, sharing ideas, learning, and collectively solving complex problems.

Professional Growth: Your growth matters to us. We provide ample opportunities for learning, training, and development. We support your aspirations and help you build a successful and fulfilling career path.

Impactful Projects: From designing renewable energy solutions to optimizing energy consumption, our projects directly impact the environment and communities. Your work will be tangible and visible, making you a catalyst for change.

Inclusive Culture: We value diversity and inclusivity. Every individual's uniqueness is celebrated and respected. We foster an environment where you can be yourself and thrive.

Current Openings

Explore our current job openings to find the perfect fit for your skills and aspirations. Whether you're a data scientist, geologist, project manager, or creative thinker, we have opportunities that align with your expertise.

How to Apply

If you're excited to be part of a forward-thinking company that's shaping the future of businesses through data-driven innovation, we encourage you to apply for our open positions. To apply, please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your motivation for joining us.

Benefits

We offer a competitive compensation package that includes wellness benefits, flexible work arrangements, opportunities for professional development, and more. At our company, your well-being and growth are our priorities.

Note: Deivoc is an equal-opportunity employer. We encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds and experiences.
